Visceral Design

The focus of this section of the Style Guide is ensuring the user has a good first impression of the site.

This page explores how the site’s visual design creates an immediate emotional impression through colours, typography, and layout.

Page Layouts

Below are low-fidelity wireframes for key pages (Homepage, Content Page, and Interactive Page). These layouts prioritize clear hierarchy, prototypicality, and mobile-first responsiveness.

Colour Scheme

Background

Secondary
#EBE3D5
Primary

The colour palette was selected to evoke warmth, calmness, and clarity. Contrast ratios have been tested to ensure legibility and WCAG AA compliance.

Typography

  • Headings (h1–h2): Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif – 2rem/700, clear hierarchy.
  • Body text: Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif – 1rem/400, optimized for readability.
  • Captions: 0.875rem/300, subtle guidance for assistive clarity.

Typography is chosen for high readability and visual consistency. Headings establish clear hierarchy, and generous line-height aids scanning.
