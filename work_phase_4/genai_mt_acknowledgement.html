GenAI Acknowledgement

Acknowledgement of GenAI Use

This acknowledgement explains my use of generative AI (GenAI) tools during the development of this project for DECO7140.

I used ChatGPT (OpenAI's GPT-4o model) and Google Gemini at several stages of the design, implementation, and writing process.

Content Generation

  • Community Feed: Prompts were used to generate example profiles and background stories for the hard-coded sample profiles.
  • News Section: Prompts were used to generate example news articles displayed in the Tech News section.
  • Images: GenAI was used to create suitable images for the site, in order to avoid any potential copyright issues with photos I do not own the rights to.

Coding Support

  • ChatGPT was used to assist with debugging code and identifying potential errors (such as subtle typos in large code files).
  • I used GenAI to clarify concepts in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — for example, by asking prompts such as: "Explain the use of HTML, CSS and JavaScript together to me as if I was 10 years old." This creation of metaphors and simple explanation highly enhances my understanding.
  • Guidance was also sought for best practices related to ARIA labels and WCAG 2.2 accessibility standards.

Writing Support

  • I used ChatGPT-4o to revise and improve the grammar and sentence structure of already existing written content.
  • All revisions were based on text I had originally written, and the GenAI tool was used to enhance clarity, flow, and professionalism.
  • All content was manually reviewed and revised after using AI assistance.
  • Example prompt used: "Improve the grammar and sentence structure of this section to make it clearer and more professional while keeping the meaning the same."

Reflection

I found GenAI to be a valuable support tool in this project. It enhanced my productivity — particularly for debugging and learning — and supported creativity when generating example content and imagery. It also helped me improve the clarity and professionalism of my written work.

I see GenAI as a collaborative partner in the design process when used critically. However, I remain fully responsible for all content, design decisions, and code quality. I am mindful of the limitations of AI, including potential biases and the risk of over-reliance, and I applied critical judgement to all AI-generated outputs.

Challenges and Strategies

While using GenAI tools, I encountered several challenges. One key challenge was ensuring that suggestions for ARIA labels and accessibility improvements actually aligned with WCAG 2.2 standards, as AI recommendations were sometimes incomplete or outdated. To address this, I consulted additional authoritative resources and manually tested my site using accessibility tools.

Another challenge was maintaining my own voice and tone in written content, as GenAI sometimes produced overly generic or formal language. I addressed this by carefully reviewing and editing all revised text to ensure it remained clear and aligned with the project's style and goals.

Finally, while GenAI was helpful in identifying potential coding improvements, it occasionally suggested solutions that were not fully compatible with my existing architecture or CSS structure. I overcame this by thoroughly testing all code in context and selectively applying only those suggestions that fit my design.

Integrity Statement

I affirm that my use of GenAI tools in this assessment fully adheres to the GenAI and MT Usage Framework for DECO7140. All outputs were critically evaluated and refined, and the final submission reflects my own work and understanding, in line with the principles of academic integrity.
