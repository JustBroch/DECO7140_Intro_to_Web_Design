GenAI and Machine Translation Acknowledgement

GenAI Acknowledgement Content

Acknowledgement of GenAI/MT Use

I acknowledge that I have used Generative AI (GenAI) and Machine Translation (MT) tools, specifically ChatGPT, during the development of this project. All use has been in strict accordance with the GenAI and MT Usage Framework and the principles of academic integrity for this course.

Completed GenAI/MT Usage Checklist

  • Declared use of GenAI/MT on this page.
  • Used GenAI for creative support and technical guidance, not for assessed critical thinking or final decision making.
  • Critically evaluated and refined all AI-generated outputs.
  • Ensured all content aligns with my personal understanding and academic expectations.

Reflection on GenAI/MT Usage

Creativity, Technical Proficiency, and Problem-Solving

GenAI tools supported my creativity by helping to refine textual content for various sections of the site, including the About page, values statements, and user-facing descriptions. It also contributed to improving my technical proficiency through guidance on CSS layout strategies and reusable helper classes for spacing and typography.

Challenges and How They Were Addressed

One challenge was ensuring that AI-generated text maintained an appropriate tone, particularly regarding inclusivity and accessibility. Additionally, I needed to carefully review AI-suggested CSS code to ensure compatibility with my mobile-first, responsive design. I addressed these challenges through iterative editing and cross-referencing with my style guide and accessibility requirements.

Evaluation and Refinement Strategies

To ensure quality, I reviewed all AI-generated content multiple times, compared it with authoritative sources, and ensured that it fit the site’s voice and ethical design principles. For technical code, I tested each suggested snippet and only integrated elements that aligned with my overall architecture and quality goals.

Impact on Productivity

GenAI greatly improved my productivity, especially when generating CSS utilities for spacing (e.g., m-1, p-x-3), refining hover effects, and drafting initial explanatory content. This allowed me to focus more time on accessibility, dynamic functionality, and user experience refinement.

Insights for Future Professional Use

Through this project, I gained valuable insights into how GenAI can serve as a collaborative partner in the design process when used critically. In future projects, I plan to continue using GenAI as a tool to enhance creativity and efficiency while maintaining full accountability for final outputs. I will also remain mindful of transparency, ethics, and potential biases in AI-generated content.

Integrity Statement

I affirm that my use of GenAI and MT tools in this assessment fully adheres to the GenAI and MT Usage Framework. All outputs were critically evaluated and refined, and the final submission reflects my own work and understanding, in line with the principles of academic integrity.
