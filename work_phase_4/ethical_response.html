Ethical Response

This page addresses the ethical implications of the client’s request to implement a newsletter subscription plug-in. The response is grounded in the ACS Code of Ethics and the structured ethical reasoning framework from the course.

Context and Ethical Concerns

The client aims to build user engagement and community through a newsletter feature. However, this raises key ethical issues:

  • Privacy: Collecting email addresses requires responsible data handling and clear communication on storage and usage.
  • Consent: Users should be informed in plain language and provide active, unambiguous consent.
  • Trust: Design should avoid dark patterns or coercive opt-ins that damage credibility.

Ethical Reasoning

  • Client’s Goal: Strengthen user retention and communication through a recurring newsletter.
  • Stakeholders: Users (including minors or marginalised groups), site maintainers, developers, and funders.
  • ACS Principles: Primacy of Public Interest, Enhancement of Quality of Life, Honesty.
  • Consequences: Poor implementation may lead to disengagement, legal breaches, or brand damage.
  • Alternatives: Implement opt-in with full transparency, offer unsubscribe functionality, and include a privacy FAQ.

Recommendation

The plug-in may be implemented if it follows ethical design principles:

  • Use a clear double opt-in confirmation process.
  • Offer a brief, friendly summary of how emails will be used.
  • Include an accessible way to unsubscribe at any time.

This solution supports user autonomy and strengthens long-term trust in the platform.

Draft Client Response

Dear Client,

Thank you for your suggestion to include a newsletter subscription plug-in to support user engagement. We agree this feature could be beneficial for long-term community building.

However, our analysis highlights important privacy and consent considerations. To ensure alignment with best practices, we recommend using a transparent, opt-in system with clear explanations and user-friendly unsubscribe options.

This will enhance trust and demonstrate a commitment to ethical design. Please let us know if you’d like to explore implementation options further.
