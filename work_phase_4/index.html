LoopedIn - Shaping a digital space for tech innovators and communities!

This website is a style guide for a proposed platform aimed at the Tech and Innovation Community. It outlines key design choices that will form the foundation of the final product – a professional social network for innovators.

Inspired by platforms like LinkedIn, this site targets users in AI, robotics, software development, and emerging tech, providing space to share knowledge, connect, and collaborate.

This style guide demonstrates key components such as typography, color usage, buttons, and layout patterns, all carefully designed to ensure accessibility, consistency, and usability across the site. These design decisions are based on client goals and user needs identified during earlier phases.
