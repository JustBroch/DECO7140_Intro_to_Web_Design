Ethical Response

This page outlines the ethical considerations behind the site’s design, such as inclusivity, accessibility, and respect for users’ data and identity.

Accessibility & Inclusion

The design aims to be inclusive of users with varying abilities. Key measures include high-contrast color palettes, scalable fonts, alt-text for all media, and semantic HTML. These ensure accessibility for screen readers and users with visual impairments.

Transparency and Consent

The platform respects user autonomy by avoiding manipulative design patterns ("dark patterns") and ensuring that actions like account creation or data sharing are clearly explained. Clear navigation, predictable layouts, and honest labeling support user trust.

Data and Privacy

While this prototype does not process user data, the design considers how data *would* be treated in a live product. Privacy-by-design principles are considered, such as user control over information and the minimization of tracking or unnecessary data storage.

Responsible Use of GenAI

All use of Generative AI in this project has been clearly documented and critically reviewed. AI has not replaced student effort or analysis, but was used to enhance clarity and support idea generation. This aligns with the ethical and academic guidelines of the course.

Commitment to Ethical Design

Design decisions reflect a commitment to ethics as an ongoing responsibility. The project avoids exclusionary practices and embraces the needs of diverse users—supporting both the client’s values and broader principles of responsible innovation.
